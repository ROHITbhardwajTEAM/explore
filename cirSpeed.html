CIRCULAR SPEED:

When a body takes one round of a circular path, then it travels a distance equal to its ‘circumference’ which is given by 2Sr, where r is the radius of the circular path.

The speed of a body (or object) moving along a circular path.

v=(2πr)/t

where,
r=radius
t=time
